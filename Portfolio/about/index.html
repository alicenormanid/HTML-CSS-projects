About Me!

My name is Alice and I am a Canadian web developer currently completing the last year of my Computer Science degree from the University of Calgary. I have a passion for web development and design and am self-taught in those respects.

In addition to working daily with web development tools such as WordPress, Javascript, HTML, and CSS, I am able to work in Java, C, Python, and R languages. I am very familiar with Windows, Android, Chrome OS, and Linux, and have some experience in iOS and Mac OS.

I am available to hire to create websites for your business from scratch with HTML, CSS and Javascript or with the use of WordPress. You can check out the "Projects" page to see some of my work and how it was accomplished. If that sounds like something you'd be interested in, click here to easily send me a message and I'll give you a consultation, free of charge!

As of now, I do not offer website hosting or domain names, but that is something that will be added in the near future -- stay tuned!
